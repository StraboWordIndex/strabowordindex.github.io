Strabo word index

On this website you can find a word index compiled by Peter Stork to the Greek text of Strabo's Geography as edited in the first four volumes of Stefan Radt's monumental edition of 2002-2005. The index contains all words in alphabetical order, including emendations and variants found in the apparatus criticus. Additionally, it contains all information regarding variations, citations, conjectures, etc. For more information you can read the introduction in the first document linked below.
